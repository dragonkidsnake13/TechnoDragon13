About

TechnoDragon13 is a YouTube channel created by Sarang Biju with a strong passion for technology, learning, and sharing knowledge. The channel focuses on creating simple, useful, and informative content for people who are interested in computers, mobile devices, and modern digital tools. We mainly share content related to Linux, Android, and general technology topics. Our videos are designed to help beginners understand complex topics in an easy way, while also providing useful tips and tricks for more experienced users. Whether it is learning how to use Linux commands, exploring Android features, or understanding new tools and software, our goal is to make technology easier for everyone. The channel was officially started on April 5, 2025. At the time of creating this webpage, we are still a small growing channel with 60+ subscribers. Even though we are small, we are fully committed to improving step by step. Every video we create is a learning experience for us, and we continuously try to improve the quality of our content, editing, and explanations. Our journey is not just about numbers or popularity. It is about passion, consistency, and improvement. We believe that every big creator starts small, and we are working patiently toward building a strong and helpful community over time. We also aim to make content that is practical and useful in real life. Instead of just theory, we focus on hands-on tips, real demonstrations, and easy explanations that viewers can actually apply. Our goal is to reduce confusion and help people become more confident with technology. As we continue to grow, we plan to explore more topics such as programming, software development, cybersecurity basics, and advanced Linux usage. We are always open to feedback, suggestions, and ideas from our viewers, because we believe that a community grows best when everyone contributes. At TechnoDragon13, our mission is simple: to learn, to grow, and to share knowledge with the world — one video at a time.
